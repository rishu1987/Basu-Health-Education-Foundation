Basu Health and Education Foundation (Medical Treatment)

Free Medical Camps

BHEF organizes free medical camps in rural and urban areas to provide basic healthcare services.

Essential Healthcare Services

The foundation offers primary healthcare, including consultations, diagnostics, and medications.

Financial Aid for Treatments

BHEF provides financial assistance to patients who cannot afford expensive treatments.

Awareness Programs

Regular health awareness campaigns are conducted to educate communities about preventive care.

Specialized Care

BHEF facilitates access to specialized treatments, including surgeries and chronic disease management.

Mobile Health Units

Mobile clinics are deployed to reach remote areas with limited healthcare infrastructure.

Partnerships with Hospitals

BHEF collaborates with hospitals to ensure quality care for patients.

Emergency Medical Support

The foundation provides emergency medical aid during crises or natural disasters.

Maternal and Child Health

Special programs are designed to improve maternal and child health outcomes.

Vaccination Drives

BHEF organizes vaccination campaigns to prevent communicable diseases.

Health Insurance Support

Assistance is provided to enroll eligible families in health insurance schemes.

Free Physiotherapy Camps

BHEF organizes camps to provide free physiotherapy services to those in need.

Rehabilitation Programs

The foundation offers rehabilitation services for patients recovering from surgeries, injuries, or disabilities.

Chronic Pain Management

Physiotherapy is used to manage chronic pain conditions like arthritis, back pain, and joint issues.

Post-Stroke Rehabilitation

BHEF provides physiotherapy to help stroke patients regain mobility and strength.

Pediatric Physiotherapy

Specialized care is offered for children with developmental delays or physical disabilities.

Geriatric Care

Physiotherapy services are tailored for elderly patients to improve mobility and quality of life.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Athletes and individuals with sports-related injuries receive specialized care.

Home-Based Physiotherapy

For patients unable to visit clinics, BHEF arranges home-based physiotherapy sessions.

Awareness on Physiotherapy

Educational programs are conducted to highlight the importance of physiotherapy in recovery and wellness.

Training for Local Physiotherapists

BHEF provides training and resources to local physiotherapists to enhance their skills.

Use of Modern Equipment

The foundation ensures access to modern physiotherapy equipment for effective treatment.

Collaboration with Physiotherapy Colleges

BHEF partners with educational institutions to promote physiotherapy as a career.

Community-Based Rehabilitation

Programs are designed to integrate physiotherapy into community health initiatives.

Mental Health and Physiotherapy

BHEF emphasizes the role of physiotherapy in improving mental health and reducing stress.

Research and Development

The foundation supports research to improve physiotherapy techniques and outcomes.
