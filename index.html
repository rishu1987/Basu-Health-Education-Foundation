About Us

Basu Health and Education Foundation

BHEF is committed to transforming lives through quality healthcare and education. We strive to make healthcare accessible and education attainable for underprivileged communities, empowering them for a better future. Through our initiatives, we promote well-being, knowledge, and self-sufficiency, fostering a healthier and more educated society.

Arvind Srivastava, Founder

Basu Health and Education Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to enhancing healthcare and education for underprivileged communities. Our mission is to ensure that quality medical services and learning opportunities are accessible to all, empowering individuals to lead healthier, more self-reliant lives. Through health camps, awareness programs, scholarships, and skill development initiatives, we work towards creating a society where no one is deprived of essential healthcare or education due to financial constraints. Our commitment lies in driving sustainable change, fostering growth, and uplifting lives through meaningful interventions. Together, we can build a healthier, more educated future. Join us in making a difference!

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team members are the backbone of our foundation. Get to know them better!

Arvind Kumar Srivatava

Founder of BHEF

Arvind Kumar Srivastava is a visionary leader with a passion for serving those who serve the nation. With years of experience in social work and community development, he founded BHEF (Brave Hearts Empowerment Foundation) to create a platform that supports soldiers and their families. His unwavering commitment to their welfare has been the driving force behind this initiative.

Rishu Srivastava

Director

Rishu Srivastava is a dynamic leader with a strong focus on youth engagement and community outreach. His innovative ideas and passion for social change have helped BHEF connect with a wider audience, fostering a sense of collective responsibility toward our soldiers.

Priyanka Srivastava

Director of Education

Priyanka Srivastava brings a wealth of expertise in organizational management and strategic planning. Her dedication to empowering soldiers and their families has been instrumental in shaping the foundation's programs. She is deeply committed to ensuring that the voices of ordinary soldiers are heard and their needs are addressed.

Dr. Anshu Srivastava

Director of Health Programs

Dr. Anshu Srivastava is a respected professional with a background in healthcare and social advocacy. Her focus on the mental and physical well-being of soldiers has led to the development of impactful health initiatives under BHEF. She believes in creating a holistic support system for soldiers and their families.
